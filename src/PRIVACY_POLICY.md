# Privacy Policy for BibleSocial

**Last Updated:** May 5, 2026

## 1. Introduction

BibleSocial ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and mobile application (the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Authentication & Account Data**
- Full name, email address (required for account creation and authentication)
- Profile information: biography, country, timezone, theme preferences, language selection
- Profile photos (up to 6 images)

**User-Generated Content**
- Posts, comments, and social interactions
- Prayer requests and prayer engagement
- Journal entries and spiritual reflections
- Bible verse highlights, notes, and saved verses
- Goals and habit tracking data
- Study plans and reading progress

**Communication Data**
- Direct messages between users
- Group announcements and discussion content
- Bible study session chat messages

**Payment Information**
- We do not directly collect payment card information. Payment processing is handled by [Base44 Payments], our third-party payment processor. Your payment details are securely processed by [Base44 Payments] in compliance with PCI DSS standards.
- We store: checkout session ID, subscription status, subscription ID, and billing email address

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage & Analytics**
- Pages visited, features used, session duration
- Device information: OS, browser type, IP address, device model
- Interactions: button clicks, form submissions, content engagement

**Device Information**
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Device identifiers
- Geolocation (if location services are enabled)

**Cookies & Tracking**
- Session cookies for authentication
- Preference cookies for theme, language, and navigation settings
- Analytics data via standard web tracking

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

- **Service Delivery:** Account management, authentication, content delivery, group/community features
- **Personalization:** Theme preferences, language selection, navigation layout, content recommendations
- **Analytics & Improvement:** Understanding usage patterns, identifying bugs, enhancing features
- **Communication:** Responding to inquiries, sending notifications (prayer reminders, daily encouragements, reading reminders — if enabled)
- **Payment Processing:** Billing, subscription management, order fulfillment
- **Legal Compliance:** Compliance with applicable laws, fraud prevention, account security

## 6. User Rights & Data Access

### 6.1 Right to Access
You can request a complete copy of your personal data by contacting [support@biblesocial.com].

### 6.2 Right to Delete
You can delete your account and associated data at any time via Settings > Account. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

### 6.3 Right to Export
Upon request, we can provide your data (posts, journal entries, goals) in a portable format. Email [support@biblesocial.com].

### 6.4 Right to Correct
You can update your profile information directly in the app via Settings.
